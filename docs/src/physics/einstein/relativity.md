# Theory of Relativity

Speed of light is constant which was predicted by the James Clerk Maxwell, whereas Isaac Newton gave a classical mechanics named Galilean relativity (theory of motion) while tells if you throw a ball at 10m/s inside a train moving at 20m/s then ground observer sees 30m/s.

If Newtonian physics predicts light speed should not be constant ( if light moves at speed c and u mode toward it then you should measure something like c+v) but that is not what happens.

Michelson-Morley experiment proved that no change in the speed of light, no matter Earth's motion.

Einstein later made two bold statement; laws of physics are the same in all inertial frames and Speed of light is constant for all observers.

To keep light speed costant, nature adjusts time these; Time slows down (time dilation), Length contracts and simultaneity becomes relative

Core idea of Newton and Einstein are:

1. Newton: Time is absolute and speed changes (even for the light). e.g. We see light beam moving slower if we chase it.
2. Einstein: Speed of light is absolute and Time changes. e.g. We still see light at speed of c though we chase a light beam at speed of light.

## Time Dilation

It is change in time when an object approach near speed of light (for that object the time of the outside world becomes very fast meaning if can travel 1 year further from only 10 sec of travel in speed of light).

Photon distance travel delay is what we see, but this is not time dilation (time dillation is what actuallly happens physically). Even if we could magically observe instantly(no delay), time dilation world still be there.

So, it is not illusion rahter it is real difference in how time flows.
